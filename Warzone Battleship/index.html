How to Play

  1. Place your ships on the grid. This is typically done by clicking on the grid squares to position your ships vertically or horizontally.
  2. Once you have set up your fleet, the game will begin.
  3. In turns, you will select grid squares to target. This is how you try to "hit" the AI's ships.
  4. After each turn, the game will provide feedback indicating whether you've hit a ship, missed, or sunk a ship.
  5. Keep taking turns until you have successfully sunk all of the AI's ships. You win the game!
