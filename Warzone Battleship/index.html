How to Play

  1. Place your ships on the grid. This is typically done by clicking on the grid squares to position your ships vertically or horizontally.
  2. Once you and the AI have set up your fleets, the game will begin.
  3. In turns, you and the AI will select grid squares to target. This is how you try to "hit" each other's ships.
  4. After each turn, the game will provide feedback indicating whether you've hit a ship, missed, or sunk a ship.
  5. Keep taking turns until you or the AI has successfully sunk all of your opponent's ships. The first one to do so is the winner.
